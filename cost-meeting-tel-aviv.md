### What is the goal?

To meet core developers of the OpenPTV, to start the project, to deliver first user-friendly versions of OpenPTV in Matlab, C++ and Python. 

### Where?

Tel Aviv University

### When? 

April 29 - May 3, 2013

### Want to participate?

Use our [mailing list](https://groups.google.com/forum/#!forum/openptv)
