4 Mozart 1.3.0

Mozart 1.3.0 is both the development and maintenance release. The work on 1.3.0 began immediately after 1.2.0, while the subsequent 1.2.* releases were intended primarily as maintenance releases. The Mozart system that became 1.3.0 has been extensively (and successfully) used for our own research projects.
